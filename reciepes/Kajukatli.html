Kaju katli

Ingredients

  • Cashew
  • Sugar Syrup

Reciepe

  1. Take 200gm Cashew and blend it to make powder
  2. Add Sugar to non sticky pan
  3. Add water and make sugar syrup by
  4. Add Cashew Powder to it
  5. stir it properly
  6. Pour the mixture on a butter paper
  7. After it become a little hard cut in shape of diamonds
